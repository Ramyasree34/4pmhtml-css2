Abdul Kalam Profile

Dr. Abdul Kalam, often referred to as the "Missile Man of India," was a renowned scientist and the 11th President of India. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he played a pivotal role in India's space and defense programs. As a key figure in the development of India's nuclear capabilities, he contributed to the country's scientific and technological advancements. Dr. Kalam was also a beloved leader, known for his humility, vision, and dedication to education. His book, Wings of Fire, inspired millions, and his philosophy of "Dream, Dream, Dream" continues to motivate people globally.

His Famous Quotes:

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
"You have to dream before your dreams can come true."
"If you fail, never give up because F.A.I.L. means 'First Attempt In Learning'."
